Hex Tabletop Demo

How to Play

  1. At the start of your turn you automatically draw 3 terrain cards (deck: 2 Grass, 2 Sand, 2 Water; reshuffle when empty).
  2. Select one of your pieces (Player 1: Blue, Player 2: Red).
  3. Highlighted adjacent hexes are those whose terrain matches at least one card in your hand.
  4. Click a highlighted hex to move there, consuming one matching card from your hand.
  5. When out of cards (or earlier if you wish) press End Turn. Then the other player draws and goes.

Goal (example): Reach the opponent's starting side or just experiment.

Prototype demo. Use and extend freely.
